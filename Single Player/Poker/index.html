How to Play 5-Card Draw Poker

  • You and the computer are each dealt 5 cards
  • Click on cards you want to replace (up to 5 cards)
  • Click "Replace Cards" to get new cards
  • Click "Reveal Hands" to see who wins
  • Best poker hand wins the round!
